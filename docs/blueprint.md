# **App Name**: Serralheiro Pro

## Core Features:

- Hero Section Display: Showcases the main product proposition with a strong headline, supporting subheadline, a prominent call-to-action button, and instant access information. Features an industrial background image relevant to metalworking.
- Visual Proof Gallery: Displays a grid of high-quality, real images of gates, grilles, metallic doors, and structures, demonstrating concrete examples of available projects.
- Problem/Solution Section: Clearly outlines the practical benefits of the product by addressing common challenges faced by metalworkers, such as saving time and increasing service offerings.
- Product Offerings List: Lists the specific components and advantages included in the product package, emphasizing organization and variety.
- Added Value Insights: Provides practical, no-nonsense tips beyond just the projects, such as pricing advice, client presentation strategies, and popular service ideas.
- Testimonials Display: Features simple, authentic testimonials from users highlighting the product's real-world utility and effectiveness.
- Flexible Pricing Plans & FAQ: Presents clear pricing tiers (Basic and Complete), prominently highlighting the 'Mais Escolhido' plan, along with a transparent 7-day money-back guarantee and an important FAQ section covering common inquiries about access, experience, device compatibility, and payment.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual scheme aims for a rugged and direct feel. A deep, robust primary color of Dark Orange (#EE8E26) is chosen for emphasis on calls-to-action and key information, reminiscent of heated metal or critical alerts. The background, a muted, desaturated Charcoal Brown (#26211B), provides a strong contrast and a sense of depth and durability. An accent color of Fiery Red (#C63012) will highlight specific elements and provide dynamic contrast.
- A single sans-serif font, 'Inter', is recommended for both headlines and body text. Its neutral, modern, and highly readable characteristics align with the target audience's preference for objectivity and practical communication.
- Icons should be clean, functional, and directly related to metalwork and construction tools. Prefer outline or solid filled icons in a flat, monochromatic style, primarily using the accent and primary colors.
- The layout is clean and sectioned, adopting an 'industrial' aesthetic without being overly decorative. Strong visual separation between sections, ample whitespace, and direct, left-aligned text blocks ensure high readability. Call-to-action buttons are oversized and centrally prominent for clear visibility and interaction.
- Subtle, non-distracting animations are applied sparingly. These include smooth transitions for section reveals on scroll and discreet hover effects on buttons, reinforcing interaction without drawing attention away from the practical content.
